Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2026  ·  Effective immediately

Plain summary: We collect what we need to run the service — your email, payment info (handled by Stripe, not us), and basic usage logs. We don't sell your data. We don't run ad tracking. Here's the full picture.

1. Who We Are

MomentumScan is operated by Anwar Ismaili as an independent software product. When this policy refers to "we," "us," or "our," it means Anwar Ismaili / MomentumScan. For privacy inquiries, contact us at privacy@momentumscan.com.

MomentumScan is an educational stock scanner. We are not a financial institution. We do not hold, transfer, or custody your money.

2. What We Collect

2.1 Account Information (via Clerk)

When you sign up, our authentication provider Clerk collects:

  • Email address (required)
  • Password hash (never stored in plaintext — Clerk handles this)
  • If you use Google OAuth: your Google-provided name, profile picture URL, and email
  • Account creation timestamp and last sign-in timestamp
  • Clerk-generated user ID (used internally to link your account to our database)

2.2 Billing Information (via Stripe)

Payment is processed exclusively by Stripe, Inc. We never see or store your full credit card number, CVV, or bank account details. What we do receive from Stripe:

  • Stripe Customer ID
  • Subscription status (active, trialing, past_due, canceled)
  • Last 4 digits of card on file (for display in your account settings)
  • Billing period start and end dates
  • Payment history (successful charges, failed attempts)

2.3 Scanner Usage Logs

When you use the scanner, we log:

  • Which setup types you viewed and when
  • Tickers added to your watchlist
  • Alert configurations you create
  • Backtester queries you run (setup type, date range — not financial decisions)
  • API request timestamps and response codes (for debugging and reliability)

We use this data to understand which features are useful and to debug problems. We don't sell it or share it with advertisers.

2.4 Technical Metadata

Our server and infrastructure automatically collect:

  • IP address (logged per request, used for rate limiting and abuse detection)
  • Browser type and version (from User-Agent header)
  • Operating system
  • Referring URL (if you arrived from another page)
  • Timestamps of all requests

2.5 Email Communications

If you're on the Free Tier, you receive a daily digest email. We track whether emails are delivered (via our transactional email provider), and we may track open rates in aggregate — not individually linked to your identity for advertising purposes. You can unsubscribe from digest emails at any time via the unsubscribe link in each email.

5. Cookies

At launch, MomentumScan uses only functional cookies necessary to operate the Service:

  • Session cookies: Set by Clerk to maintain your authenticated session. These expire when you sign out or when the session token lifetime is reached.
  • Preference cookies: If we store any UI preferences (e.g., selected setup view) in your browser's local storage. Not transmitted to third parties.

We do not set advertising cookies, third-party tracking pixels, or cross-site tracking cookies. We do not use Google Analytics or Facebook Pixel.

Your browser can be configured to block or delete cookies. Blocking functional session cookies will prevent you from logging in.

6. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data retained as long as your account exists.
  • Canceled accounts: We retain account data for 90 days after cancellation in case you wish to reactivate, then delete or anonymize it.
  • Billing records: Retained for 7 years to comply with financial record-keeping requirements.
  • Server logs (IP, request metadata): Retained for 30 days, then deleted.
  • Stripe data: Subject to Stripe's own retention policies, which we cannot unilaterally control.

Regardless of where you live, you can always:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and associated personal data (subject to our retention obligations above).
  • Export: Request your data in a portable format (CSV or JSON).

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@momentumscan.com with the subject "Privacy Request." We will respond within 30 days. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling a request.

8. Children's Privacy

MomentumScan is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us at privacy@momentumscan.com.

9. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • All data in transit is encrypted via TLS (HTTPS).
  • Passwords are never stored — authentication is handled by Clerk, which uses industry-standard hashing.
  • Payment card data never touches our servers — Stripe handles tokenization and PCI compliance.
  • Our Postgres database on Railway is not publicly accessible; connections require credentials and are within a private network.
  • API keys and secrets are stored as environment variables, not in source code.
  • Access to production systems is limited to the operator (Anwar Ismaili).

No system is perfectly secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we will act promptly if a breach occurs.

10. Data Breach Notification

If we become aware of a data breach that may affect your personal information, we will:

  • Notify affected users by email within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach (consistent with GDPR Article 33 standards, applied as a baseline);
  • Describe the nature of the breach, the data affected, likely consequences, and steps we are taking;
  • Notify applicable regulatory authorities as required by law.

1. Free Trial Period

All new Pro subscribers receive a 7-day free trial. During the trial:

  • You have full access to all Pro features (full scanner, watchlist, backtester, alerts).
  • No payment is collected from your card.
  • You may cancel at any time during the trial at no cost.

When the 7-day trial period ends, your subscription automatically converts to a paid monthly plan and your card is charged $39.00. To avoid this charge, cancel before the trial ends.

2. Canceling During the Trial (No Charge)

How to cancel your trial before being charged:

  1. Sign in to your MomentumScan account.
  2. Go to Account → Billing (or navigate directly to the Customer Portal link in your welcome email).
  3. Click "Manage Subscription" — this opens the Stripe Customer Portal in a new tab.
  4. In the Customer Portal, click "Cancel Plan".
  5. Confirm the cancellation. You'll receive a confirmation email from Stripe.

Your trial access remains active until the trial end date. After that, your account automatically downgrades to the Free Tier (daily email digest, basic view).

3. Refunds After First Charge

If you were charged for a month of Pro access and decide it's not right for you, we'll consider a pro-rated refund if you request it within the first 14 days of the billing cycle in which the charge occurred.

3.1 Eligibility

  • Request must be submitted within 14 calendar days of the charge date.
  • Refunds are pro-rated based on unused days remaining in the billing cycle at the time of the request.
  • Refund requests are evaluated on a case-by-case basis. We reserve the right to decline requests that appear abusive (e.g., repeated trial + refund cycling).
  • This policy applies to the first paid cycle. Subsequent renewal charges are not eligible for refunds.

3.2 How to Request a Refund

Email billing@momentumscan.com with:

  • Subject line: Refund Request — [your email address]
  • The email address on your MomentumScan account
  • A brief reason for the request

We will respond within 3 business days. If approved, the refund is processed via Stripe and typically appears on your statement within 5–10 business days depending on your card issuer.

4. No Refunds After 14 Days

After the 14-day window following a paid charge, we do not offer refunds. Subscription fees are non-refundable once the 14-day period has passed.

This applies to:

  • Monthly renewal charges (after the first billing cycle)
  • Charges where the 14-day window has expired
  • Situations where the account was used during the billing period

If you no longer wish to be billed, cancel your subscription (instructions below). Cancellation stops future charges — it does not generate a refund for the current period.

5. Canceling Your Subscription (Paid)

How to cancel your paid subscription:

  1. Sign in to your MomentumScan account.
  2. Go to Account → Billing.
  3. Click "Manage Subscription" to open the Stripe Customer Portal.
  4. Click "Cancel Plan".
  5. Select "Cancel at end of period" (recommended — you keep Pro access through the end of the period you've already paid for).
  6. Confirm. You'll receive a Stripe confirmation email.

After canceling, your Pro access continues until the end of the current billing period. On the next renewal date, instead of charging you, Stripe will downgrade your account to the Free Tier. You will not be charged again unless you resubscribe.

6. Refund Processing

All refunds are processed through Stripe, the same payment processor that handled your original charge. MomentumScan does not handle payment card data directly.

Once a refund is approved:

  • Stripe initiates the reversal to your original payment method.
  • Timing depends on your bank or card issuer — typically 5–10 business days.
  • You will receive an email from Stripe confirming the refund.

If you paid via Apple Pay or Google Pay, refunds are returned to the underlying card associated with those services.

7. Exceptional Circumstances

If you experienced a technical issue that prevented you from accessing the Service during a paid period, or if you were charged in error, contact us at billing@momentumscan.com and we will investigate promptly. We want to be fair.
